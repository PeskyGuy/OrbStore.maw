About This Game

In a world where souls can be bound together through ancient ritual, your choices determine not just your fate — but the fate of every companion who walks beside you. Soulbound Legacy is a 80+ hour RPG where relationships are your greatest weapon.

The Soul-Bond System:
Form deep connections with your party members through dialogue, shared combat, and moral choices. Each bond unlocks unique combination abilities and story branches. But bonds can also break — betray a companion's trust and they may turn against you.

A World in Transition:
Set 50 years after the founding of the Union, Ohsa is a nation still healing. The scars of the Civil Centennial War run deep. Travel between Magus strongholds and former Tekk territories, navigating prejudices that refuse to die.

Key Features:
• 12 recruitable companions, each with full personal questlines
• Dual protagonist system — switch between two linked heroes
• Turn-based tactical combat with soul-bond combo moves
• Multiple endings based on bond strength and story choices
• Fully voiced dialogue (over 40,000 lines)

Note: The controversial ending has been hotly debated on the Orb Community forums. We stand by our creative vision. — Soulforge Games
