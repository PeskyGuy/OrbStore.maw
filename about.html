About Orb Digital Distribution

Our Mission

Orb was founded by visionary enchanter Gabriel Gear, who began his journey in 1982 A.U. with a small physical storefront in the Vyrodin industrial district known as Gear's Arcane Goods. Originally a shop for physical spell-scrolls and enchanted machinery, Gear anticipated the digital revolution of the Mass Arcane Web (M.A.W.) and transitioned the company to the Union's first digital distribution network in 1998 A.U.

Today, backed by the Vyrodin Technological Institute, Orb remains dedicated to Gear's original vision: to bridge the gap between magic and digital accessibility. We believe that whether you are a Magus in the High spires or a Tekk in the lower districts, great gaming should be only a click away.

Technology

Powered by the revolutionary ArcX technology, Orb utilizes the Union's global V-Link network to deliver high-fidelity mana-data directly to your Arcslate or Personal Grimoire. Our proprietary distribution compression allows for massive titles like Soulbound Legacy to be downloaded in mere hours, rather than days.

Our Values

Accessibility: We support all modern Arcslates, Personal Grimoires (PG), and vintage Tekk-era terminals.
Security: Your Union-denominated transactions are protected by Level 7 Ethereal Encryption.
Growth: We host over 4,000 titles from major publishers and indie spellsmiths alike.

Orb is proud to be a subsidiary of Vyrodin Global Holdings. For partnership inquiries, please visit our offices in the Holy District, Plaza of Progress.
