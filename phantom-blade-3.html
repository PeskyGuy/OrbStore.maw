About This Game

The Phantom returns. Years after the events of Phantom Blade 2, a new threat emerges from within the Church of Embrace itself. As Agent Kael, you must infiltrate the most guarded institutions in Ohsa to uncover a conspiracy that could reshape the continent.

Arcane Blade Combat:
Master the signature Phantom Blade — a mana-infused weapon that exists between dimensions. Phase through walls, strike from the shadows, and vanish before your enemies can react. New in PB3: dual-blade stances and arcane chain attacks.

Infiltration Reimagined:
Every mission offers multiple approach paths. Scale the spires of the Holy District, crawl through Undercity tunnels, or disguise yourself as a Church official. The world reacts to your methods — go loud and future missions become harder.

Key Features:
• 16-chapter campaign across Vyrodin and two new cities
• Completely overhauled stealth AI with sound propagation and light awareness
• New skill tree: Void Arts — manipulate shadows and mana fields
• Challenge mode: timed assassination contracts with global leaderboards
• Pre-order bonus: exclusive "Nightwalker" blade skin
