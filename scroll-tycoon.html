About This Game

The MAW's industrial heartland is yours to command. Scroll Tycoon: MAW Empire is a factory-building and logistics simulation set in the Union's sprawling underground refineries. Start with a pickaxe and a dream — end up running the most sophisticated arcane manufacturing operation in the known world.

Manual Extraction to Full Automation:
Mine raw ferrite and crystal shards by hand to bootstrap your first Union Marks, then reinvest into Mining Rigs, Refineries, Forges, and Assemblers to build self-sustaining production lines.

Directional Conveyor Networks:
Route resources across the factory floor using rotatable conveyor belts and storage silos. Design efficient supply chains or watch your output grind to a halt.

Tiered Arcane Manufacturing:
Smelt raw ore into Ferrite, Copperium, and Gildite. Refine raw crystals into Quartz, Ruby, and Diamond. Combine processed materials in your Forge and Assembler to produce ArcM slates and, ultimately, the legendary Grimoire — worth 100,000 UM on the open market.

Global Market Leaderboard:
Your Union Marks score is tracked against the great industrial CEOs of the MAW. Can you unseat Gabriel Gear from the top spot?
