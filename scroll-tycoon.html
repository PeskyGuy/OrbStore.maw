About This Game

Scroll Tycoon: Arcane Trading Simulator is a roguelite stock trading game set in the Free District of Vyrodin, 2005 A.U. You're a trader operating in the underground markets, dealing in scrolls, grimoires, mana crystals, spellarms, and their components.

Seasonal Trading:
Survive 120 days across four seasons (Spring, Summer, Autumn, Winter). Each season increases difficulty - fewer items in the market, higher volatility, and more dangerous events.

Real-Time Market:
Prices update every 20 seconds. Watch the countdown timer and time your trades carefully. Market conditions change constantly - what's profitable now might crash in moments.

Reputation System:
Build reputation through successful trades, then spend it on powerful abilities: negotiate discounts, find premium buyers, gain insider information, or call in Church protection.

Illegal Trading:
High-risk, high-reward illegal items appear in the market. Stolen spellarms, contraband crystals, and forbidden grimoires sell for massive profits - but every transaction adds heat. Reach 100% heat and the Church Guard arrests you. Game over.

Interactive Events:
Random events force difficult choices. Deal with mysterious traders, desperate sellers, Church inspections, mana surges, and more. Your decisions affect your marks, reputation, heat, and inventory.

Contracts & Rumors:
Accept lucrative contracts to deliver specific items by deadline. Gather market rumors to predict price spikes. Use mana abilities to manipulate the market.

Global Leaderboard:
Compete against other traders. Will you retire wealthy or get arrested trying?
