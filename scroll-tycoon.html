Scroll Tycoon: M.A.W. Empire

Build your Arcslate manufacturing empire and dominate the Mass Arcane Web market in this business simulation

About This Game

The M.A.W. (Mass Arcane Web) has transformed society. What was once a luxury for the wealthy is now essential—everyone needs an Arcslate to function in modern Ohsa. Messages, banking, identification, even ration cards are all digital now. And you? You're going to get rich selling them these devices.

Start with a small workshop in the industrial district of Vyrodin. Hire enchanters, source raw materials, and design your first Scroll model. Will you focus on budget devices for the masses, or luxury Arcslates for the elite? Maybe you'll corner the black market with untraceable models that bypass Church surveillance?

Build Your Manufacturing Empire:
Manage every aspect of production—from mining arcane crystals to final assembly. Upgrade your facilities, research new technologies, and optimize your supply chain. Hire skilled workers: enchanters to imbue the devices with magic, engineers to design sleeker models, and salespeople to push your products across all nine districts of Vyrodin.

But business isn't just about production. Navigate corporate espionage, bribe Church officials for lucrative contracts, and sabotage your competitors. The market is cutthroat—literally. Some rival CEOs have been known to "disappear" after undercutting the wrong people.

Control the M.A.W. Infrastructure:
It's not enough to make Scrolls—you need to control the network itself. Build M.A.W. relay towers, establish data centers, and create your own apps and services. Charge subscription fees, sell user data (carefully—the Church watches), and become the backbone of digital life in Ohsa.

Key Features:
• Design custom Arcslate models with different specs, features, and price points
• Manage complex supply chains across multiple districts with varying regulations
• Research new technologies: longer battery life, better encryption, holographic displays
• Navigate political intrigue—work with or against the Church and underground factions
• Dynamic market simulation where your decisions affect prices and demand
• Expand beyond Vyrodin to other cities in Ohsa
• Sandbox mode: unlimited resources to build your dream tech empire
• Campaign mode: rise from a small workshop to a continental monopoly
