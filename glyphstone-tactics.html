About This Game

Glyphstone Tactics is a turn-based strategy game where positioning is everything. Maneuver your units across battlefields embedded with ancient glyphstones — each granting unique buffs, debuffs, or special attacks to those who hold them.

Master the Grid:
Command a diverse army of knights, mages, and mythical beasts. Use the environment to your advantage: push enemies into lava, bridge gaps with earth magic, and claim high ground for tactical superiority.

Key Features:
• Deep turn-based strategy with hexagonal grid movement
• 50+ unique unit types with upgradeable skills
• 25 story-driven campaign missions
• Local and online multiplayer modes
• Custom map editor for endless replayability
