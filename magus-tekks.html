Magus and Tekks

Intense tactical FPS combat where magic meets military technology in a war-torn world

About This Game

MATURE CONTENT - Contains intense violence, blood, and strong language

The streets have become a battlefield. Rogue mages clash with military enforcers equipped with anti-magic technology. You are an elite operative caught in the crossfire, wielding both arcane spells and cutting-edge weaponry to survive.

Fully Fledged Campaign - 10 Chapters:
Experience an epic story spanning multiple war-torn cities. From gleaming corporate districts to lawless slums, each chapter takes you deeper into the conspiracy that threatens global stability. Uncover the truth behind a shadowy organization while fighting for survival against impossible odds.

Dual Combat System:
Master both magical and technological warfare. Cast devastating spells with your left hand while firing high-tech weapons with your right. Combine fireballs with grenades, use telekinesis to throw enemies into your line of fire, or cloak yourself with illusion magic while flanking with a plasma rifle.

Choose your loadout before each mission: Evocation magic for raw damage, Transmutation for defensive barriers, or Divination to see enemies through walls. Pair these with assault rifles, sniper rifles, shotguns, or experimental arcane-tech hybrid weapons.

Multiplayer Mayhem:
Take the fight online with up to 16 players. Team Deathmatch, Capture the Relic, and Siege modes across 12 diverse maps ranging from urban battlegrounds to mystical ruins. Customize your operative with hundreds of spell and weapon combinations.
