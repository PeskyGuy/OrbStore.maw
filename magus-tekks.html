Magus and Tekks

Tactical Combat — Clash of ideologies between the Church of Embrace and the Industrialists.

[Screenshot Area: Magic vs Tech]

About This Game

The Civil Centennial War may have ended, but the conflict between tradition and progress still burns in the heart of Vyrodin. On one side, the Church of Embrace wields ancient magic and calls upon divine protection. On the other, the Industrialist Union builds massive steam-powered golems and utilizes the latest in Tekk engineering.

Take command of a strike team and navigate the dangerous streets of the Free District. Will you join the Church to preserve the sacred balance of Ohsa, or will you stand with the Union to build a future free from divine interference?

Tactical Combat & Strategy:
Every move counts. Use the environment to your advantage — find cover in industrial ruins or utilize ley lines to boost your magical abilities. Combine the strengths of different units to overcome overwhelming odds in deep, turn-based tactical battles.

Key Features:
• Choose between magic-focused Church units or technology-focused Union units
• Dynamic battlefield environment with destructible cover and environmental hazards
• Deep unit customization: upgrade equipment, learn new abilities, and specialize your team
• Narrative-rich campaign mode spanning Vyrodin's history and the roots of the ideology clash
• Local and online multiplayer support for competitive tactical play
